Lucidity is a dream journaling and lucid dreaming application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using Lucidity, you agree to the practices described in this policy. Information we collect Account information When you create an account, we may collect: ✦ Email address ✦ Authentication identifiers ✦ Account preferences and settings Dream journal data When you use Lucidity, you may choose to store: ✦ Dream entries ✦ Dream titles ✦ Dream notes ✦ Tags and classifications ✦ Progression and achievement data ✦ Reality check settings and preferences ✦ How we use information We use information to: ✦ Provide and maintain the app ✦ Synchronize data across devices ✦ Restore data when users change devices ✦ Improve reliability and performance ✦ Respond to support requests ✦ Protect the security of user accounts We do not sell personal information. We do not use dream content for advertising purposes. We do not use dream content to train artificial intelligence models. ✦ How dream data is stored Lucidity is designed as a local-first application. Dreams are stored on your device and synchronized across your devices using encrypted cloud backups. Before dream content leaves your device, it is encrypted and then stored on Lucidity's cloud infrastructure to enable synchronization, backup, and account recovery. Dream content is not routinely accessed, read, or reviewed by Lucidity. ✦ Cloud services Lucidity uses trusted third-party infrastructure providers to support authentication, storage, synchronization, notifications, and related services. These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services. ✦ Security Protecting dream privacy is a core design goal of Lucidity. We implement technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect user data. ✦ Account recovery Users may recover access to their account using supported authentication methods such as email verification. Account recovery is designed to restore access to synchronized data without requiring users to manage encryption keys manually. ✦ Data export Users may export their dream journal data using features provided within the app. Exported data is provided in a readable format for the user. ✦ Account deletion Users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the app. When an account is deleted: ✦ Account information may be removed ✦ Synchronized dream data may be permanently deleted ✦ Recovery may no longer be possible Certain backup copies may persist temporarily as part of standard infrastructure retention and disaster recovery processes. ✦ Children's privacy Lucidity is not directed toward children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to remove that information. ✦ Changes to this policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will update the date shown at the top of this document. Continued use of Lucidity after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. ✦ Contact For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us. Yours, Saga
